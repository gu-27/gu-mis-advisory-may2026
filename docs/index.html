Program Updates

Where We've Been

Curriculum Restructure
We've completed a meaningful restructure of our curriculum. The core fundamental skills — data, programming, systems analysis — are now condensed into a tighter, more integrated foundation. Most importantly, we've reduced the overlap between concentrations, creating clearer differentiation between MIS and Business Analytics and opening space for emerging technology coursework.

Looking Ahead

"The hardest part — restructuring the curriculum — is largely behind us. The next chapter is about identity, positioning, and preparing graduates for a market that's shifting faster than ever."
The Market Reality
  • Tougher entry-level job market for graduates
  • Industry professionals losing roles, increasing competition
  • Tools lowering the barrier to entry — but raising the bar for differentiation
"We will not give up the fundamentals. They are why our students last. But we have a real opportunity to equip students with emerging skills that let them differentiate — to come in not just credentialed, but ready to add value in ways their more experienced peers may not yet."
